### 1  Board, Square IDs, Pieces
* 8×8 board. Files **a–h** (White’s left → right); ranks **1–8** (nearest → farthest).
* Piece letters (FEN / SAN): **K Q R B N P** for White, lowercase for Black.

### 2  FEN Anatomy
A legal FEN has **six** space-separated fields  
`<pieces> <side> <castling> <ep> <halfmove> <fullmove>`

| Field | Example | Meaning |
|-------|---------|---------|
| pieces | `rnbqkbnr/pppppppp/8/.../RNBQKBNR` | 8 ranks, ‘/’-separated, top (8) → bottom (1) |
| side   | `w` / `b` | side to move |
| castling | `KQkq`, subset, or `-` | K=White O-O, Q=White O-O-O (k,q for Black) |
| en-passant square | `e6` or `-` | target square **immediately** after a 2-square pawn push |
| halfmove | `0`… | ply since last pawn move or capture (50-move rule) |
| fullmove | `1`… | increments **after** Black’s move |

### 3  Piece Movement & Capture
* **King** 1 square any direction; cannot move into check.  
* **Queen** any squares orthogonally/diagonally.  
* **Rook** any squares orthogonally.  
* **Bishop** any squares diagonally.  
* **Knight** (L-jump) (±2, ±1) or (±1, ±2); may jump over pieces.  
* **Pawn** one square forward; **two** squares from its initial rank if clear; captures one square diagonally forward.  
  * **Promotion** to **Q R B N** on last rank.  
  * **En passant** capture: if an enemy pawn has just advanced two squares and stops adjacent, you may capture **as if** it had moved only one. Right expires after the next half-move.

### 5  Check, Mate, Draw
* **Check**  — king attacked; must be removed immediately.  
* **Checkmate** — king in check **with no legal move**.  
* **Stalemate** — side to move has **no legal move** yet king **not** in check.  
* Draw may also be claimed by:  
  * **Fifty-move rule** (50 full moves without a pawn move or capture)  
  * **Threefold repetition** (fields 1-4 identical, same side to move, three times)  
  * **Insufficient mating material** (e.g. K vs K, K B vs K, K N vs K)  
  * **Mutual agreement**
